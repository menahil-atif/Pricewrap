How we Promote Sustainability

Each sale product on our website is accompanied by a sustainability rating to provide transparency and empower you to make informed choices.

“The best way to predict the future is to create it.” - Peter Drucker

The rating takes into account: manufacturing processes, materials used, energy consumption, waste management, labor practices, fair wages, working conditions, diversity and inclusion and animal welfare considerations.
